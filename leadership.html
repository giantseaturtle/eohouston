Leaders serving leaders

Volunteer leadership is where EO gives back the most.

Every EO leader is a volunteer and a fellow business owner. Stepping up to serve the chapter is one of the fastest ways to grow as a leader. You build confidence, expand your global network, and help shape the future of EO. As one leader puts it: “the more I give, the more I receive.”

1

Chapter Leadership

Serve on the EO Houston board or lead a function or committee, sharpening your team-building and community skills right here at home.

2

Regional Leadership

Help design the EO experience across multiple chapters, gain cross-cultural reps, and broaden your influence well beyond Houston.

3

Global Leadership

Shape EO’s global strategy, finances, and key initiatives while leading international teams of fellow entrepreneurs.

★

Global Leadership Conference

Step onto the board and you earn a seat at the GLC, EO’s annual world gathering of leaders for training, networking, and immersive learning in a new destination each year.

A perk of stepping up

From the boardroom to Harvard, Wharton & beyond.

Leadership opens doors. Members and leaders get access to EO Executive Education, world-class programs built for entrepreneurs with busy lives and a thirst to keep learning:

  • EO with Harvard Business School: creative strategy for evolving industries.
  • EO@Wharton: elevating finance and operations.
  • EO/London Business School & EO@INSEAD: global growth and team leadership.
  • Entrepreneurial Masters Program and the Global Speakers Academy.
