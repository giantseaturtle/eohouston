Find your people. Grow your business.

EO Houston membership is for established business owners who are ready to learn, connect, and grow alongside Houston’s best entrepreneurs.

Do you qualify?

Membership requirements

EO membership is reserved for entrepreneurs who lead established companies. You’re likely a fit if:

  • You are the founder, co-founder, owner, or controlling shareholder of your company.
  • Your business grosses at least US $1 million in annual revenue.
  • You’re ready to share openly and give back, not to sell or be sold to.
  • You want a trusted group of peers who will push you to grow.

What membership gives you

Why entrepreneurs join EO Houston

EO isn’t a networking group or a lead source. It’s a community built to make you a better leader and your business stronger, alongside peers who’ve been exactly where you are.

◎

Your own Forum

A confidential group of 6–10 fellow owners who meet monthly and become your most trusted personal board of advisors. For most members, it’s the single most valuable part of EO.

✦

World-class learning

75+ events a year with speakers and workshops chosen by entrepreneurs, for entrepreneurs: practical insight on scaling, finance, leadership, marketing, exit, and life.

✺

A global network

Instant access to 19,000+ members across 200+ chapters in 80+ countries, plus MyEO communities built around your industry and your passions.

◆

A safe room to be real

A strict non-solicitation policy means nobody is selling. Members share the real numbers, the hard decisions, and the personal stuff they can’t talk about anywhere else.

✷

Once-in-a-lifetime experiences

Signature socials, family days, the annual chapter retreat, and bucket-list experiences you simply can’t buy a ticket to, shared with people who become lifelong friends.

★

A better life, not just a bigger business

EO is designed to grow the whole entrepreneur. You’ll walk away with perspective, accountability, and momentum in business and in life.
