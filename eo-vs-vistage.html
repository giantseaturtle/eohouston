EO Houston VS Vistage

Both put you in a room of fellow leaders, but the Entrepreneurs’ Organization (EO) Houston and Vistage are built differently. Here is how they compare, and how to join each.

Many members belong to both.In Houston, owners often join the Entrepreneurs’ Organization (EO) Houston for themselves and put their senior executives in Vistage. Here’s how the two compare so you can decide what fits.

Vistage is a chair-led executive coaching group focused squarely on business performance. Groups usually run 16 to 22 people, meetings typically take your whole day, and it isn’t built to be a social organization.

EO Houston is a member-led founder community that grows the whole entrepreneur, business and life, wrapped in once-in-a-lifetime experiences.

How to qualify for each

You can join both, and many owners do. Here is what it takes to get into each, and where to start.

EO

Apply to EO Houston

You’re the founder, owner, or controlling shareholder of a company grossing $1M+ a year (or $2M+ in venture funding with 10+ employees). Apply, meet a member, and experience the chapter first.

Start your application
V

Apply to Vistage

You’re a CEO, business owner, or key executive. You join a local peer group led by a Vistage Chair (a paid executive coach), often with companies in the $1M–$5M+ range, by connecting with a local Chair. Groups usually run 16 to 22 people, and monthly meetings typically require your whole day.
