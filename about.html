About

A global network. A Houston family.

EO Houston is the local chapter of the Entrepreneurs’ Organization, a peer community where business owners help each other become better leaders and live richer lives.

Who we are

Entrepreneurs helping entrepreneurs.

Founded in 1987, the Entrepreneurs’ Organization is a global, peer-to-peer network of more than 19,000 influential business owners across 200+ chapters in over 80 countries. EO exists for one reason: to help entrepreneurs learn and grow from each other.

EO Houston brings that mission home. We are founders and CEOs of established companies who gather to share hard-won experience, learn from world-class speakers, and build the kind of trusted relationships you simply can’t find anywhere else. We come to give as much as we come to gain.

How EO works

Three ways we grow together

◎

Forum

Every member joins a Forum: a small, confidential group of 6–10 fellow owners who meet monthly. Using EO’s experience-sharing protocol, members speak only from personal experience, never giving advice. It becomes your most trusted personal board.

✦

Learning

Members-only learning events, workshops, and speakers chosen by entrepreneurs, for entrepreneurs. With 75+ events a year spanning scaling, finance, leadership, marketing, exit, and personal growth, always practical and immediately useful.

✺

Connection

Signature socials, family events, and bucket-list experiences across Houston and beyond. Plus access to 200+ EO chapters worldwide and MyEO communities organized around shared passions and industries.

Our culture

A safe room, built on trust.

EO is not a networking group and it is not a place to sell. The chapter operates under a strict non-solicitation policy: members never pitch, sell, or market to one another. That single rule is what makes the room safe.

Because no one is selling, members open up about the things they can’t share anywhere else: the real numbers, the hard decisions, the personal stuff. We share experience, not advice, so everyone leaves with perspective instead of pressure. That is the EO difference.
