EO Houston VS TIGER 21

TIGER 21 is about protecting wealth. The Entrepreneurs’ Organization (EO) Houston is about creating it. Here is how the two compare, and how to qualify for each.

Different stages, same caliber of peers.The Entrepreneurs’ Organization (EO) Houston is for founders still building; TIGER 21 is for preserving wealth after an exit. Many leaders do both over the arc of a career.

TIGER 21 is a peer community for ultra-high-net-worth individuals focused on portfolio defense, preserving and allocating capital, often after a major exit. EO Houston is for founders who are still building.

As TIGER 21 itself frames it, EO is where you learn to create success, and TIGER 21 is where you decide what to do with what that success created.

How to qualify for each

Many leaders do both, at different stages. Here is what it takes to get into each, and where to start.

EO

Apply to EO Houston

You’re the founder, owner, or controlling shareholder of a company grossing $1M+ a year (or $2M+ in venture funding with 10+ employees). Apply, meet a member, and experience the chapter first.

Apply to TIGER 21

You have $20M+ in investable assets or qualifying net worth and pass TIGER 21’s vetting on character, contribution, capacity, conditions, and capital. Most members have already had a major liquidity event.
