What is EO?

The world’s most influential community of entrepreneurs.

The Entrepreneurs’ Organization is a global, peer-to-peer network of more than 19,000 business owners. EO Houston is our local chapter: founders and CEOs who come together to share real experience, learn from world-class speakers, and support one another through the highs and lows of building a business.

Members lead companies that have crossed seven figures in revenue, and they come to give as much as they get.

The EO difference

A safe room, built on trust. Strictly no soliciting.

EO is not a networking group, and it is not a place to sell. Our chapter operates under a strict non-solicitation policy: members never pitch, sell, or market their products or services to one another. That single rule is what makes the room safe, and it’s why members open up about the things they can’t share anywhere else.
