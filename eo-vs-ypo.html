EO Houston VS YPO

Two of the most respected peer communities for business leaders. Here is an honest comparison of the Entrepreneurs’ Organization (EO) Houston and YPO, including how to qualify and apply to each.

Many of our members are dual members.A large number of Entrepreneurs’ Organization (EO) Houston members also belong to YPO. This isn’t an either/or, and many tell us EO Houston is the more relatable room, with founder peers and roughly half the cost.

EO and YPO share a lot: confidential peer forums, a focus on the whole leader, and a calendar full of unforgettable experiences.

The biggest differences come down to who qualifies, what it costs, how relatable the room feels, and how hard it is to actually get in.

Bottom line:If you run a much larger company and want a room of big-company chief executives, YPO is a great fit, if you can get in. Houston membership is strongly gated, and most new joiners start in a regional forum with real travel. If you’re a founder or owner who wants relatable peers, about half the cost, a local seat you can actually get, and a membership that’s yours for as long as you’re in business and beyond, EO Houston is hard to beat. Many members happily belong to both.

How to qualify for each

You can absolutely belong to both, and many Houston leaders do. Here is what it takes to get into each, and where to start.

EO

Apply to EO Houston

You’re the founder, owner, or controlling shareholder of a company grossing $1M+ a year (or $2M+ in venture funding with 10+ employees). Apply, meet a member, and experience the chapter before you join.

Start your application
YPO

Apply to YPO

You hold a qualifying chief executive role (president, CEO, or chair) at a company that meets YPO’s size thresholds, and members typically join before their late 40s. Membership is by application and sponsorship. Know that the Houston chapter is strongly gated: many members are grandfathered in, and new applicants usually have to start in a regional forum, which may meet only four to five times a year and can involve a lot of travel.
