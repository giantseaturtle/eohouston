What to expect

The kinds of events you’ll experience

Membership opens the door to a full calendar, from world-class learning to once-in-a-lifetime experiences, both here in Houston and around the globe.

◎

Learning Events

EO Houston brings the world’s leading speakers and visionaries to your doorstep. Gain access to the world’s top experts and grow beyond your personal limits.

✦

Social Events

A space where members have real conversations and learn from one another by sharing their experiences, their successes, and their failures.

✺

Family Events

Full-featured events supporting members with families, with family-friendly venues and activities with fun for the young, the old, and the young at heart.

◆

Full Chapter Events

Chapter-wide events that generally include spouses and significant others. The most common are holiday parties, end-of-year celebrations, and more.

✷

Global Events

Learn from today’s leading business minds at global events, networking with like-minded peers while enjoying once-in-a-lifetime experiences in unique destinations.

★

Once-in-a-Lifetime Events

Quite literally once in a lifetime. Imagine an intimate group of fellow CEOs hearing a former president share candid, jovial stories about world-impacting decisions.

◈

Multi-Chapter Events

Organized around specific themes or experiences, these offer a rich environment for networking, building alignment between chapters and across the region.

◎

Regional Events

Connect with EO members from across the region at events that broaden your network and perspective well beyond Houston.

✦

Community & Mentors

Give back and grow by mentoring, and being mentored by, fellow entrepreneurs across the EO community, on and off the calendar.
