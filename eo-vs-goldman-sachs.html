EO Houston VS Goldman Sachs10,000 Small Businesses Program

The Goldman Sachs 10,000 Small Businesses program is a fantastic launchpad, but it ends. The Entrepreneurs’ Organization (EO) Houston is the community that keeps going, for as long as you own a business.

A program vs. a community.Goldman Sachs 10,000 Small Businesses is a brilliant short program; the Entrepreneurs’ Organization (EO) Houston is a community for life. Many founders do 10KSB first, then join EO Houston as they scale.

Goldman Sachs 10,000 Small Businesses is a tuition-free education program: a focused curriculum, a cohort, and a graduation. It’s a brilliant on-ramp for businesses of every size.

EO Houston is different. It’s an ongoing membership, more like a gym than a class: you get out what you put in, month after month, for as long as you’re in business.
